Project #1

36 Rep Max Calculator

This was my first project which included HTML, CSS with Bootstrap, and Vanilla Javascript. I've also recently made some small changes with Typescript. I often used a 20 rep max calulator during or outside of my strength training workouts to figure out my 1 rep max each time to plan my appropriate sequential weights and reps for the next workout. This is the first website that I know of that calculates both bodyweight and external weight with reps to calculate one rep max as well as extend past 20 reps.

Note: This was the first website I've ever hosted. I use it at least a few days a week and has proven to be very useful.
